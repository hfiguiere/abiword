Inserting objects into AbiWord

In order to streamline the creation of your document, AbiWord allows you to insert preset objects.

Inserting Date and Time

The current date and time can be inserted into your document automatically. Position your cursor where you want to insert the date, and go to the "Insert" menu. Click on "Date and Time" .

The window seen below will appear:

This window contains several different formats for dates and hours. Choose the desired format and click on the "OK" button or click on the "Cancel" button to cancel

Inserting special characters

To insert special characters that do not appear on the keyboard, position the cursor at the place you wish to insert it, and got to the "insert" menu. Then clcik on " Special Characters... " (or using the keyboard short cut , to simultaneously press the keys " Ctrl " and " M ").

The window below will then appear:

Select the character font on the top by clicking on arrow at the topmost field.
Then click on the grid, and select the desired character.
When this character is enlarged down the bottom, click on "Insert" to insert the character. Repeat as many times as is necessary.
When you are finished, click on the "Close" button.

Inserting an image

It is possible in Abiword to insert images into your documents. The image format that you can use is the *.png (Portable Network Graphics) format.

To insert an image, position hte cursor, where you wish the picture to go, and goto the "Image menu". Click on "Image..."

The window seen below will appear:

Then enter(or browse to) the path where your image is and click on "Open " button to insert the image.

AbiWord regards this inserted image as a character. So it is subject to existing alignments and line spacing, and can be moved by spacing or tabs.
