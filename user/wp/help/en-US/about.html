About abisource.com

This Web site is the central " hub " of activities and exchanges for the AbiSource project. It is the means for you communicate with us and we hope that you will take advantage of it. Certain sections of this site are especially dedicated for the developers of AbiWord. Other sections are for the users. Wether you are a user or developer, we regard you as an integral part of our community and we want the communication in this community to be as open as the software is.

You will also be able to find on this site " merchandise " like tee-shirts and DS to support the effort of the project as well as articles relating to AbiSource and AbiWord in particular.

 

 

Please visit the abisource.com site to download the lastest version of AbiWord and to get the lastest news.
