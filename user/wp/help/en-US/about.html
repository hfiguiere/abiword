About abisource.com

Abisource.com is the central hub of activity and communication for the AbiSource project.

It is the place to find the the lastest version of AbiWord, articles relating to the project and AbiWord T-Shirts. There are sections designed specifically for software developers and other sections designed for users. You will find a variety of ways to communicate with users, contributors and developers of AbiWord. We want the communication to be as open as the software itself.

The AbiSource project is sponsored by SourceGear Corporation.
