Tabs

Tabs make it possible to autmatically align several different lines or elements into columns, used for example, to simulate a table. There are two types of tabs: the default tabs and personalised tabs.

Default Tabs

To use the default tabs, simply press the "tabs" key on the keyboard. The cursor will then move along 1/2 inch (according to the ruler).

Setting Tabs

They make it possible to choose the place and the alignment of the tabs. Here are the various types of personalised tabs available in Abiword :
* Left tab   The left end of the text is aligned to the tab

* Center tab  The center of the text is aligned to the tab

* Right tab  The right end of the text is aligned to the tab

* Decimal tab   The decimals of a number are aligned to the tab

To select them, click on the tab selector   on the left hand side of the ruler. Once the tab is selected, click once on the rule where you wish the tab appear. The tab then appears on the ruler.

To align the text with this tab, position the cursor to type in the text that you wish to align, and then press the "tab" key. 

To vary the personalised tab, click above the ruler and move the tab to it's new position.

To remove the personalised tab, click above the ruler and move the tab out of it.
